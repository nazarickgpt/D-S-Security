Welcome to D&S Security Specialists

We are a compliant South African security-services provider offering guarding, patrol, access-control, and specialist services to commercial and residential clients in KwaZulu-Natal and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to provide reliable, professional security services that protect lives and assets, build trust, and deliver peace of mind to clients.

Our vision is to become the preferred security partner in the KwaZulu-Natal region, known for compliance, high-standards, and tailored solutions.

Values: Integrity, responsiveness, professionalism, client-focus, continuous improvement.

About Us

D&S Security Specialists is based in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. Directed by Nkonzo Dlamini and Ndumiso Zama , both holding certified qualifications .

We are registered with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) and the Private Security Industry Regulatory Authority (PSiRA). We ensure full compliance with South African regulations, including tax registration (SARS), UIF, COIDA, and necessary insurances.

Our Unique Selling Proposition

We differentiate by combining local management in Durban/KZN, directors with certified credentials, a strong commitment to training and responsiveness, and personalised service rather than “one-size-fits-all”. This focus on client-specific needs and ongoing improvement helps us outperform generic providers.

SWOT Analysis

  • Strengths: Directors’ certifications, local focus, compliance-ready.
  • Weaknesses: New entrant; less brand recognition.

  • Threats: Strong incumbents; regulatory/insurance risk; staff turnover.

Services Offered

Each service is backed by trained licensed personnel, compliant with the regulatory regime in South Africa.

  • Static guarding of premises (commercial, residential estates)
  • Mobile patrol & response services
  • Access control and gatehouse operations
  • Risk assessment & security consulting
  • Event security (crowd control, access)
  • Optional future expansion: CCTV/monitoring, electronic access systems

Target market segments include commercial clients (retail outlets, small offices, warehouses), residential estates and sectional titles in Durban/KZN, and events (corporate gatherings, functions).
